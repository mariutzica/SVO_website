mechanical_impedance

property

Definition: Mechanical impedance is a measure of how much a structure resists motion when subjected to a harmonic force. It relates forces with velocities acting on a mechanical system. The mechanical impedance of a point on a structure is the ratio of the force applied at a point to the resulting velocity at that point.Mechanical impedance is the inverse of mechanical admittance or mobility.
