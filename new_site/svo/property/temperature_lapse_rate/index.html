property

Definition: The lapse rate is the rate at which an atmospheric variable, normally temperature in Earth's atmosphere, falls with altitude. Lapse rate arises from the word lapse, in the sense of a gradual fall. In dry air, the adiabatic lapse rate is 9.8 째C/km (5.4 째F per 1,000 ft). At Saturated Air Lapse Rate (SALR), where value is 1.1 째C/1000ft - 2.8 째C/1000ft as obtained from ICAO.
