dibutyltin-diacetate

matter

Definition: Dibutyltin dilaurate (abbreviated DBTDL) is an organotin compound with the formula (CH3(CH2)10CO2)2Sn((CH2)3CH3)2. It is a colorless viscous and oily liquid. It is used as a catalyst .
